Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 2025
Puntly ("we", "our", or "us") is a social betting club app that lets friends share bets, track performance, and compete on leaderboards.
What we collect
•	Account information — email address and display name when you sign up
•	Profile data — nickname you choose for each club
•	Bet data — bet details you enter or photograph, including stake, odds, selection, and outcome
•	Photos — bet slip images you choose to upload from your camera or photo library
•	Device token — a push notification token to send you bet reminders (only with your permission)
•	Timezone offset — used solely to send reminders at an appropriate local time
How we use it
We use your data only to operate the app — showing your bets to club members, calculating leaderboards, and sending reminders you've opted into. We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties outside of the services that run the app (Firebase/Google, for authentication, database, and push notifications).
Data storage
Data is stored securely using Google Firebase, hosted in the United States. Bet slip images are stored in Firebase Storage.
Your rights
You can delete your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. Club admins can remove members from their club.
