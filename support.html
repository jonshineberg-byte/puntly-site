Getting Started

Creating a club

  1. Tap "Create club" on the welcome screen
  2. Give it a name
  3. Share the invite code with your group

Joining a club

  1. Tap "Join club"
  2. Enter the invite code from your club admin

Adding a bet

  1. Tap the camera button to photograph a bet slip (we'll read the details automatically)
  2. Or use "Enter manually"

FAQs

Can I be in multiple clubs?
Yes — tap your club name at the top of the screen to switch between clubs.
What's a bonus bet?
When adding a bet, select "Bonus bet" if the stake came from a bookmaker promotion rather than your own money. P&L is calculated accordingly (no stake returned on a win).
What's a club bet?
A bet attributed to the club as a whole rather than an individual member — useful for syndicate bets.
How do I leave a club?
Long-press your name in the Club tab and select "Leave club". Your bets remain in the club history.
I'm not receiving notifications
Check that notifications are enabled for Puntly in your iPhone Settings → Notifications.
