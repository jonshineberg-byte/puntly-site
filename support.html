Getting Started

Creating a club

  1. Tap "Create club" on the welcome screen
  2. Give your club a name
  3. Share the invite code with your group

Joining a club

  1. Tap "Join club"
  2. Enter the invite code from your club admin

Adding a bet

  1. Tap the camera button to photograph a bet slip — we'll read the details automatically
  2. Review and confirm, or adjust any fields
  3. Or tap "Enter manually" to fill in the details yourself

FAQs

Can I be in multiple clubs?
Yes — tap your club name at the top of the screen to switch between clubs.
What's a bonus bet?
Select "Bonus bet" if the stake came from a bookmaker promotion. P&L is calculated accordingly — the stake is not returned on a win.
What's a club bet?
A bet attributed to the club as a whole rather than an individual member — useful for syndicate wagers.
How do I leave a club?
Go to the Club tab, tap the three-dot menu, and select "Leave club". Your bets remain in the club history.
I'm not receiving notifications.
Check that notifications are enabled for Puntly in iPhone Settings → Notifications.
How do I report a user or content?
Long-press any message or comment to reveal the Report and Block options.
